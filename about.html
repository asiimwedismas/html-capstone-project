Zrolls World Tour 2020

The Zrolls World Tour, A joyful music festival believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people and trolls from over 100 countries is taking place this December, in Uganda.
This year, we hope to expand our invitation list to include other franchises and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the casue of the Zrolls, free music and open knowledge about the strings.
