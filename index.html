Featured speakers

Queen Poppy

Queen of the pop trolls

POPPY is our lead, a relentlessly upbeat - if slightly naive - Troll whose father King Peppy saved his subjects from the Troll-hungry Bergens.

CREEK

All about positivity

CREEK is the most positive, supportive, reassuring Troll in all of Troll Village, approaching everything with Zen like wisdom. He’s calm, collected and capable.

DJ SUKI

Music guru

DJ SUKI can always be counted on to lay down some beats for an impromptu musical moment — of which there are many in Troll Village.

GUY DIAMOND

Confidence wizard

GUY DIAMOND is our resident “naked glitter Troll,” with heaps of body confidence and a unique idea of personal space. He is a party on two feet.

GRANDMA ROSIEPUFF

Grandma Rosiepuff keeps young trolls safe so they can learn to let their true colors shine!

KING PEPPY

Braver than a lion

KING PEPPY, as the brave leader of the Trolls, led his people on a torch-lit escape to freedom from Bergen Town. His heroism and valor were the stuff of Troll legend. Quick with inspirational words of wisdom, King Peppy ushered in a new era of happiness and security in Troll Village.
